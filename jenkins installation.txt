how to change port using commands:-
---------------------------------

sudo sed -i -e 's/^\s*#\s*AmbientCapabilities=CAP_NET_BIND_SERVICE/AmbientCapabilities=CAP_NET_BIND_SERVICE/' /usr/lib/systemd/system/jenkins.service
